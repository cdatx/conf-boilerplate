Container Days Austin 2015

March 27 and 28, 2015, Hawaiian Falls, Pflugerville

Only $60 Register now!

About

Container Days is an un-conference style gathering meant to discuss the revolution of Docker, CoreOS and other light weight virtualization technologies and their applications.

Patterned closely after CitCon and DevOps Days, Container Days provides a forum for all those interested in all the technical, process and production ramifications of adopting container style virtualization.

Registration will be opening soon...

Location

It may seem crazy, but this water park is the ideal space for an unconference. A beautiful great room, solid internet access and break out rooms will allow attendees to spread out and truly take advantage of the un-conference format.

It also doesn't hurt that Hawaiian Falls is providing a free water park admission this summer for each participant! Use some other confernence's t-shirt to keep the sun off!

18500 N SH 130. Pflugerville, Texas

Schedule

Container Days Austin happens Friday night and all day Saturday.

Friday night includes a great talk from TBA. We will enjoy hors d'oeuvres and drinks while conducting the opening circle and session planning. The evening will close with a social hour.

Saturday will open with breakfast and a quick review of the un-conference rules. A great talk from TBA2 will fill minds and stomachs are filled with breakfast tacos. Jack Hates Coffee will be serving your favorite latte on site in the morning as well.

Lunch will be TBA. Attendees can choose to play in the arcade, or continue interesting conversations in the Lean Coffee style over lunch in the break out rooms.

Four open sessions across 6 locations will provide a variety of topics determined by attendees and we will close the circle.

While not officially part of the conference, the tradition of #BeerOps in Austin will happen at Shogun, just across the road from Hawaiian Falls. We will take a poll and make reservations accordingly. Come ready to socialize.
